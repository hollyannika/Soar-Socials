About Me

Hi, I’m Holly, the founder of Soar Socials. I help brands and creators cut through the noise, grow their online presence, and connect with the audiences that matter most.

With a degree in Multimedia Journalism and experience creating content that engages, informs, and inspires, I understand what makes people stop scrolling—and start clicking.

With my passion for social media management I have challenged myself to create many mock campaigns (some of which you can check out by scrolling down to projects). Through this I have developed skills in social media strategy, content creation, and audience engagement—all of which I now bring to every client I work with.

At Soar Socials, my goal is simple: to help your brand take flight. Whether it’s crafting scroll-stopping posts, building a consistent brand story, or finding the best ways to reach your audience, I make sure your socials aren’t just seen—they’re remembered.

Let’s work together to make your brand soar.
